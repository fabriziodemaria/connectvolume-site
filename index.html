ConnectVolume

Spotify Connect speaker volume,
straight from your keyboard

ConnectVolume is a macOS menu bar app that lets you control the volume of your Spotify Connect devices and Sonos speakers right from your keyboard. Your F11 / F12 keys — or your own global shortcuts — drive whatever’s actually playing, with an on-screen HUD so you always know where the volume landed.

Download for Mac

Version 0.0.6 · macOS 14 Sonoma or later · Notarized Developer ID

Spotify Connect

Control the volume of any device showing up in your Spotify Connect picker, signed in once via PKCE OAuth. Live status next to every device.

Sonos, even when Spotify can’t

Spotify’s Web API can’t set volume on most Sonos speakers, so ConnectVolume talks to them directly over SOAP/UPnP on your LAN.

F11 / F12 that does the right thing

Hardware volume keys control your remote speakers when something external is playing; fall back to the system volume when nothing is.

Global hotkeys & on-screen HUD

Configurable system-wide shortcuts with hold-to-repeat, plus a transparent HUD overlay so you always know where the volume landed.

Requirements

  • macOS 14 Sonoma+
  • Spotify Premium (for Spotify Connect)
  • Sonos on local network (optional)
  • Accessibility permission (for hardware key intercept)
